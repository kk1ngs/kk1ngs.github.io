ZENARY

TEAM: Alex Xie, Jessica-Rachel Kumar, Zaara Hussein

SOFTWARE: Rhino, Enscape, Sketchup, Photoshop

LOCATION: Waterloo Park, ON

Acting as a multipurpose leisure space and featured structure in the heart of Waterloo Park, Zenary is a mass timber pavilion designed by four Architectural Engineering students for their final studio project.
